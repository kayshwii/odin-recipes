Chicken Parmesan

How to make CRISPY chicken parmesan

I hate all of these recipe websites that are walls of text containing some life story that has nothing to do with the food.

You came here for a chicken parm recipe.

Here you go.

    Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breast halves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup bread crumbs (seasoned panko if you're fancy)
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1/2 cup olive oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup mozzerella
  • salt, pepper, italian seasoning to taste

    Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Pound chicken to an even 1/2 inch thickness.
  3. Beat eggs in a bowl. Mix bread crumbs, italian seasoning and 1/2 cup parmesan cheese into another bowl.
  4. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Coat both sides with flour.
  5. Dip a coated chicken breast into beaten eggs. Transfer the breast to the bread crumb mixture, pressing crumbs into both sides. Repeat for each breast. Set aside and let the chicken rest for 10 minutes.
  6. Heat 1/2 cup olive oil in a skillet on medium-high heat. Cook chicken in the oil until golden, about 2 minutes per side.
  7. Transfer chicken to a baking dish. Top with 2 tablespoons tomato sauce and a layer of mozzerella and parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, until the chicken is 165 degrees.
